Toys

It’s not only the kind of toys you are thinking about that are included in this category, but also video games, card games, fishing and hunting tools, sports equipment, and party decorations.

The revenue of the UK’s toys and games market in 2021 was over 7.1 billion British pounds.







The value of imported toys is rising throughout the years, from 5.18 billion pounds in 2019 to 6.98 billion pounds in 2021. Most contributions were made by imports from China, which have risen 50% in three years.

The proportion of imported toys from China ranks the highest among all seven products, taking about 66% of the UK’s total toy imports in 2021.

In comparison, China's toy exports to the UK take only about 5.2% of its total exports in this category, with a slight decline from 5.68% in 2019.
